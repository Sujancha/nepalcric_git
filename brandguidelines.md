# nepalCRIC Brand Guidelines

## Brand Name
**nepalCRIC**

Always written as one word with "nepal" in lowercase and "CRIC" in all caps. 

### Acceptable Usage
- nepalCRIC
- NEPALCRIC (only when specifically required by all-caps heading styles)

### Unacceptable Usage
- NepalCric (capital N, lowercase ric)
- nepalcric (all lowercase)
- Nepal Cric (with a space)
- nepal CRIC (with a space)

---

## Logo Usage

### Clear Space
Maintain clear space around the logo equal to the height of the "C" in the logo on all sides to ensure maximum visibility and impact.

### Minimum Size
- Digital: 120px width minimum
- Print: 1 inch / 25mm width minimum

### Logo Don'ts
- Do not stretch, squash, or distort the logo proportions
- Do not rotate the logo
- Do not change the logo colors outside of approved variations
- Do not add effects (drop shadows, gradients, harsh outlines)
- Do not place the primary logo on busy photographic backgrounds without a proper overlay

## Color Palette

### Primary Colors

**Rhino Blue**
- HEX: #1E3A8A
- RGB: 30, 58, 138
- Use as: Primary brand color, headers, main navigation, primary buttons, and key UI elements. Represents trust and authority.

**Crimson Red**
- HEX: #D32F2F
- RGB: 211, 47, 47
- Use as: Accent color, live score indicators, hover states, notifications, and 'breaking news' tags. Represents energy and passion.

### Neutral Colors

**Stadium White**
- HEX: #FFFFFF
- RGB: 255, 255, 255
- Use as: Primary backgrounds, text on dark backgrounds, and content cards.

**Light Gray**
- HEX: #F8F9FA
- RGB: 248, 249, 250
- Use as: Secondary page backgrounds, subtle dividers, and table headers.

**Dark Gray / Slate**
- HEX: #1F2937
- RGB: 31, 41, 55
- Use as: Main body text, secondary text, and dark mode backgrounds.

## Typography

### Primary Font (Headings & Display)
**Bebas Neue** (or Impact as fallback)
- Features a condensed, aggressive weight perfectly suited for a stadium/broadcast vibe.
- Use for all major headlines (H1, H2, H3) and massive metric displays.
- Always use **uppercase**.

### Secondary UI Font (Subheadings & Buttons)
**Barlow**
- Clean, structured, slightly tall sans-serif.
- Use for subheadings, button labels, tabs, and small UI callouts where Bebas is too loud but Inter is too plain.

### Tertiary Font (Body Text)
**Inter** (or Roboto as fallback)
- Highly readable standard for paragraph blocks.
- Use for article body text and regular descriptive text.

### Font Weights
- **Black (900) / Regular**: Bebas Neue (used for massive roar impact on H1).
- **Semi-Bold (600)**: Barlow (Subheadings, Buttons, Table headers).
- **Regular (400)**: Inter (Standard body text).

### Font Sizes & Hierarchy Patterns
- **H1 (The Roar)**: Bebas Neue, uppercase, tracking-tighter (`tracking-[-0.02em]`), leading-none.
- **H2 & H3 (Sections)**: Bebas Neue, uppercase, bold if applicable, leading-tight.
- **Subheadings/CTAs**: Barlow, font-semibold, tracking-wider where appropriate.
- **Body**: Inter, font-normal, 16px.

### Line Height
- Headings (Bebas): 1.0 to 1.1 (condensed)
- UI (Barlow): 1.2 to 1.4
- Body (Inter): 1.6 (to ensure easy reading of long news articles)

## UI Elements

### Buttons

**Primary Button**
- Background: Rhino Blue (#1E3A8A)
- Text: Stadium White (#FFFFFF)
- Border Radius: 8px
- Font Weight: Semi-Bold (600)
- Hover State: Snap to Crimson Red (#D32F2F) for an energetic, tactile feel.

**Secondary Button**
- Background: Transparent
- Border: 2px solid Rhino Blue (#1E3A8A)
- Text: Rhino Blue (#1E3A8A)
- Hover State: Light gray background tint (#F8F9FA)

### Cards (For News & Player Profiles)
- Background: Stadium White (#FFFFFF)
- Border Radius: 12px
- Shadow: `0 4px 12px rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.08)`
- Interaction: On hover, slight upward lift (translateY -2px) and a subtle Rhino Blue border to make the interface feel alive.

### Input Fields (Search & Forms)
- Background: Stadium White (#FFFFFF)
- Border: 1px solid #E5E7EB
- Border Radius: 8px
- Focus Border: 2px solid Rhino Blue (#1E3A8A)

## Iconography

- Style: Solid and bold, consistent stroke width to match the weight of the "nepalCRIC" text.
- Size: 24px standard, 20px small (inline UI), 32px large (dashboard metrics).
- Color: Match standard text color (#1F2937) or Rhino Blue (#1E3A8A) for active/emphasized states.
- Custom Elements: Use the red and blue cricket ball motif from the logo as custom bullet points or loading spinners where appropriate.

---

## Tone of Voice

- **Passionate & Energetic**: Write like a true fan. Hype up boundaries, celebrate wickets, and use active, punchy language (e.g., "The Roar of the Rhinos").
- **Knowledgeable & Authoritative**: When discussing history or stats, be 100% accurate. Dive deep into the data without making it sound like a boring textbook.
- **Accessible & Inclusive**: Use simple, direct language. Avoid overly complex jargon so both hardcore fans and newcomers can easily understand the content.
